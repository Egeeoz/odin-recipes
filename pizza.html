Pizza Recipe

Simple pizza recipe, with cheese and olive topping.

Ingredients

    Dough, tomato sauce, cheese and olives

Steps

  • Make dough, spread the dough to a pizza form.
  • Make tomate sauce, put the tomate sauce on the pizza dough, then sprinkle gently some grated cheese.
  • Put the pizza in the oven for 15 minutes on 200 degrees Celsius.
